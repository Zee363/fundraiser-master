Raising Spirits, Raising Funds: An Evening of Community & Impact• by Swizimtrust

More about the event

This special night to support SwiZim is not just another event on your calender; it's a journey towards creating lasting change.

By joining this unforgettable night, you will experience the power of commuinity and see how SwiZim is dedicated to using this power to uplift people in Zimbabwe through innovative and holistic approaches.

Your Ticket, Their Future - Here is What CHF 100 of Impact Can Do:

  1. Education Empowerment

    2. Empower a young girl in Zimbabwe with a year of education, books, and dreams.

  2. Agricultural advancement

    3. Enable a farmer in Zimbabwe to enroll in sustainable farming practices and hope.

  3. Health and Well-being

    4. Support 5 child-headed households in Zimbabwe with meals for a month.

  4. Digital Literacy Drive

    5. Allow a young woman to access an innovation centre with coaching and internrt access for 4months. Opening a world of digital opportunities for her.

  5. Entrepreneurial Kickstart

    6. Fuel the dreams of aspiring ebtrepreneurs in Zimbabwe by offering them starting capital.

Highlights of the Evening:

  • Inspirational talks - Be moved by stories of change and resilience.
  • Silent Auction - Bid on unique items and experiences, fostering hope and community betterment.
  • Joyful Music - Enjoy heartwarming live music with sounds from Africa to lift spirits and celebrate humanity.
  • Networking Opportunities - Connect with individuals who make a difference in an atmosphere of joy and purpose.
  • Food, Drinks & Party - Enjoy a vibrant night with exquisite selection of Zimbabwean and Swiss foods & drinks.
